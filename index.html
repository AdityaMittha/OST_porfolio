Who
I Am.

I'm a third-year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student at Walchand Institute of Technology, Solapur — the kind of engineer who gets excited about register-level debugging at 2 AM.

My core focus is embedded firmware: writing lean, reliable C for microcontrollers, wiring sensors up, and watching hardware come alive. Comfortable from bare-metal 8051 all the way up to STM32 HAL and Linux userspace.

Beyond hardware, I work with edge ML and YOLO inference, spatial audio synthesis with physics engines, AWS cloud integration, and real-time IoT dashboards. I compete at national hackathons and build things that solve real problems.
